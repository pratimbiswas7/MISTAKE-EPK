About The Song

“MISTAKE” is a dark R&B track that dives into the aftermath of a toxic love that felt impossible to escape. Built on haunting emotions and raw vulnerability, the song captures the feeling of being addicted to someone who slowly becomes your downfall. With imagery of poison in the veins, burning desire, and scars that refuse to fade, it paints a moody, late-night atmosphere where love and pain blur together. The vocals carry a sense of obsession and heartbreak, while the lyrics shift between holding on and finally letting go. “MISTAKE” is about realizing too late that the person you gave everything to was never right for you—and finding the strength to walk away from something that once felt like everything.
